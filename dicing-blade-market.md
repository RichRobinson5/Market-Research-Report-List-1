<p><p>Dicing blades are categorized into two main types: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades have a central hub that connects the blade to the dicing machine, providing stability and support during the cutting process. On the other hand, hubless dicing blades are designed without a central hub, allowing for a larger cutting area and enabling more precise and efficient cutting. Both types of dicing blades are widely used in various industries, including semiconductor manufacturing, electronics, and precision machining.</p></p>
