Adipates, trimellitates, epoxies, and benzoates are four types of non-phthalate plasticizers. Adipates are popular due to their low cost and good compatibility with other materials. Trimellitates offer excellent resistance to low temperatures and are used in applications such as automotive interiors. Epoxies provide high heat stability and are commonly used in electrical cords and cables. Benzoates are known for their high solvating properties, making them suitable for adhesives and sealants.</p></p>
