<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The endoscopic cold light market encompasses three main types: halogen lamp, optical fiber cold light source, and xenon lamp. Halogen lamps consist of a tungsten filament enclosed in a bulb filled with halogen gas, which produces a bright light. Optical fiber cold light sources use fiber optic technology to transmit light, offering flexibility and control. Xenon lamps use xenon gas to emit intense, high-quality light suitable for medical procedures. These different market types cater to specific needs in endoscopy, providing options for efficient lighting and clear visualization during procedures.</p></p>
