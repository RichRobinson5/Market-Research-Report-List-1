The market can be segmented into two types: TOF Technology 3D Sensing and Structured Light 3D Sensing. TOF Technology 3D Sensing utilizes Time-of-Flight technology to measure the distance between objects and the sensor, enabling accurate depth perception. Structured Light 3D Sensing projects a pattern of light onto the object and calculates depth based on the distortion of the projected pattern. Both technologies have significant applications in industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, healthcare, and robotics.</p></p>
