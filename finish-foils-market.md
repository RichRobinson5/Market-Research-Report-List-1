<p><p>Finish foils are widely used in the manufacturing industry for decorative purposes. Two common types of finish foils are PVC and PET. PVC finish foils are made of a highly flexible and durable plastic material, providing excellent resistance to chemicals and weather conditions. On the other hand, PET finish foils are made of polyethylene terephthalate, which offers high transparency, strength, and dimensional stability. Both PVC and PET finish foils provide the desired aesthetic appeal and protect the underlying surfaces, making them suitable for various applications in industries such as furniture, interior design, and automotive.</p></p>
