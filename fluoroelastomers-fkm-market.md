These elastomers are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and oil and gas. Fluorocarbon rubber is the most common type of FKM, known for its broad chemical resistance. Fluorosilicone rubber combines the properties of fluorocarbon rubber with those of silicone rubber, providing enhanced fuel and oil resistance. Fluorinated phosphonitrile rubber offers superior resistance to hot air, steam, and flame, making it suitable for specialized applications.</p></p>
