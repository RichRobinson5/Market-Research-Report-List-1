<p><p>Short-staple spindles refer to spinning tools used in the textile industry to create yarn from short fibers. The market for short-staple spindles can be categorized into three types. Supported spindles are mounted onto a surface or spinning wheel, providing stability during spinning. Bottom whorl drop spindles have a weighted whorl at the bottom, which aids in spinning and keeping tension. Top whorl drop spindles have a weighted whorl at the top, enabling longer spinning times. These three types cater to different spinning techniques and preferences in the textile industry.</p></p>
