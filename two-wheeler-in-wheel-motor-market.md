<p><p>The two-wheeler in-wheel motor market comprises two types: outer rotor type and inner rotor type. In the outer rotor type, the motor is installed outside the wheel rim, providing a larger torque output and higher acceleration capabilities. On the other hand, the inner rotor type features a compact design with the motor installed inside the wheel hub. This type offers better maneuverability and improved space utilization. Both market types cater to the needs of the two-wheeler industry, providing different advantages to meet various consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
