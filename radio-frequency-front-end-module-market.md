<p><ul><li>Power Amplifiers (PA)</li><li>RF Switches</li><li>RF Filters</li><li>Low Noise Amplifiers (LNA)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radio frequency front-end module market comprises various types of components. Power amplifiers (PA) are responsible for amplifying the signal strength to enhance transmission. RF switches are used for routing signals between different components. RF filters eliminate unwanted frequencies and noise from the signals. Low noise amplifiers (LNA) amplify weak signals while maintaining low noise levels. Lastly, the "others" category includes additional components like mixers, oscillators, modulators, and demodulators that play a crucial role in the overall functioning of the front-end module.</p></p>
