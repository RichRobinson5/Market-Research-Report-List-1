<p><p>Nitrogen generators are used to produce nitrogen gas in various industries. There are three common types of nitrogen generators: pressure swing adsorption (PSA), membrane, and cryogenic air. PSA generators use adsorbent materials to separate nitrogen from other gases. Membrane generators rely on selectively permeable membranes to separate nitrogen from the air. Cryogenic air generators cool the air to extremely low temperatures to separate nitrogen. These generators are widely utilized in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, petroleum, and electronics for applications like packaging, purging, and preventing oxidation.</p></p>
