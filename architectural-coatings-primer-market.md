Waterproof coatings are used to protect surfaces from water damage, preventing moisture penetration and subsequent deterioration. These coatings are commonly applied on roofs, foundations, and concrete surfaces. On the other hand, corrosion protection coatings are designed to prevent metals from rusting and corrosion. They are commonly used in industrial and marine environments where metal structures are exposed to harsh conditions such as moisture, chemicals, and saltwater. Both types of coatings provide essential protection and extend the lifespan of architectural surfaces.</p></p>
