<p><p>FPC EMI Shielding Film Market Types include conductive adhesive type, metal alloy type, and metal microneedle type. Conductive adhesive type utilizes a conductive adhesive layer to provide electromagnetic shielding. Metal alloy type consists of a layer made of metal alloys, offering high shielding effectiveness. Metal microneedle type utilizes tiny metal needles to create a conductive path for shielding. These types of films are used in flexible printed circuit boards to prevent electromagnetic interference and ensure optimal performance in electronic devices.</p></p>
