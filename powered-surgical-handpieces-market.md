<p><p>The powered surgical handpieces market encompasses three main types: electric-powered, battery-powered, and pneumatic-powered. Electric-powered handpieces are connected to an electrical source and provide continuous power for surgical procedures. Battery-powered handpieces operate wirelessly and are portable, making them suitable for procedures without access to electrical outlets. Pneumatic-powered handpieces utilize compressed air to drive their operation. Each type offers unique advantages based on power source, portability, and usage environment, catering to the diverse needs of surgeons and medical professionals during surgical interventions.</p></p>
