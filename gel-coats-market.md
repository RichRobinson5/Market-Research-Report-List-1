The increasing use of fiberglass in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction is expected to drive the demand for gel coats. Fiberglass composites offer excellent strength-to-weight ratios and corrosion resistance, making them an ideal choice for lightweight and durable applications. Gel coats play a crucial role in protecting and maintaining the integrity of fiberglass structures, thereby increasing their lifespan and reducing maintenance costs.</p><p>Moreover, the growing popularity of recreational boating and water sports activities is also likely to boost the demand for gel coats in the marine industry.
