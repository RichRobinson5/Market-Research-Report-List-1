With a construction consisting of eight strands twisted together, these ropes are commonly used in heavy-duty applications such as towing, mooring, and offshore operations.</p><p>3. 12 Strand ropes provide exceptional strength and longevity. With twelve strands braided together, they offer high load-bearing capabilities, making them ideal for demanding applications like lifting, rigging, and industrial purposes.</p></p>
