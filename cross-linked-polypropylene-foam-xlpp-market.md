<p><p>Cross-linked Polypropylene Foam (XLPP) is a type of foam material used for various applications. It can be categorized into two market types: physically cross-linked and chemically cross-linked. Physically cross-linked XLPP foam is created through mechanical processes, resulting in a closed-cell structure with excellent durability and thermal insulation properties. Chemically cross-linked XLPP foam, on the other hand, is produced using chemical agents, which offer improved flexibility, elasticity, and resilience. Both types find applications in industries such as automotive, packaging, construction, and sports due to their lightweight, shock-absorbing, and water-resistant features.</p></p>
