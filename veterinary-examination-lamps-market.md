<p><p>The veterinary examination lamps market consists of two main types: LED and Halogen lamps. LED lamps utilize light-emitting diodes to produce bright, focused light. They are energy-efficient, have a longer lifespan, and generate less heat compared to halogen lamps. LED lamps are also adjustable, allowing veterinarians to easily direct the light where needed. On the other hand, halogen lamps use a tungsten filament to produce light. While they are less energy-efficient and have a shorter lifespan, halogen lamps are often cheaper and offer a warm light that some veterinarians prefer.</p></p>
