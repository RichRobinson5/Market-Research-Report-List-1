<p><p>The telescopic sight market includes a variety of types such as collimating optical sight and reflex sight. A collimating optical sight is designed to provide a clear, magnified view of a target, allowing shooters to accurately aim and hit their desired mark. On the other hand, a reflex sight uses a reflecting glass to project a reticle onto the device's field of view, aiding in quick target acquisition without the need for precise eye alignment. Both types cater to the diverse needs of shooters, offering different advantages based on their specific applications.</p></p>
