<p><ul><li>Rubber Bellows Type</li><li>Sleeve-Style Type</li><li>Coil Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air bag suspension market offers three types of suspension systems: rubber bellows type, sleeve-style type, and coil type. The rubber bellows type utilizes flexible rubber chambers that contain compressed air to provide cushioning and support. The sleeve-style type employs a cylindrical air spring that works similarly to the rubber bellows type. Lastly, the coil type suspension system features air springs covered with a coil spring for additional stability and load-bearing capacity. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of customers looking for air bag suspension solutions.</p></p>
