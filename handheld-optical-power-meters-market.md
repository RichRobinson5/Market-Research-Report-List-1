<p><p>Handheld optical power meters are devices used to measure the power of optical signals in fiber optic networks. The market types of handheld optical power meters include single channel, 2 channel, 4 channel, and other markets. The single channel meters are designed to measure power in one specific channel, while the 2 channel meters can simultaneously measure power in two different channels. The 4 channel meters offer the capability to measure power in four channels simultaneously. The "other" market refers to handheld optical power meters that have capabilities and features beyond the conventional single, 2, or 4 channel meters, such as higher channel count or additional functionalities.</p></p>
