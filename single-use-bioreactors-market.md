<p><p>The Single-Use Bioreactors (SUBs) market includes different types of bioreactors, such as Wave-induced Motion SUBs, Stirred SUBs, and others. Wave-induced Motion SUBs use rocking motion to create a wave-like environment for cell growth, minimizing shear stress. Stirred SUBs use impellers to agitate the culture and enhance mixing. Other types of SUBs may include bubble column SUBs or packed-bed SUBs. These various types cater to different cell culture requirements and offer advantages such as ease of use, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness in the biopharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
