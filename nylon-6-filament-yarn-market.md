<p><p>Nylon 6 filament yarn market types include flat, POY, FDY, and HSO. Flat refers to a yarn with a flat cross-section, offering high coverage and smoothness. POY stands for partially oriented yarn, which is semi-crystalline and offers good strength and dyeability. FDY, or fully drawn yarn, is highly oriented and provides excellent strength, elasticity, and luster. HSO, also known as high-shrinkage nylon, is a specialty yarn that shrinks significantly when exposed to heat, commonly used in applications like textiles and industrial sectors.</p></p>
