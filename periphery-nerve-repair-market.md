<p><p>The Peripheral Nerve Repair market comprises three main types: Nerve Conduit, Nerve Wrap, and Nerve Graft. Nerve Conduit involves using synthetic tubes to bridge a gap between damaged nerves, facilitating nerve regeneration. Nerve Wrap utilizes a protective covering around the damaged nerve, providing a conducive environment for nerve regrowth. Nerve Graft involves transplanting a healthy nerve from another part of the body to repair the damaged nerve. These techniques aim to restore nerve functionality and improve patient outcomes for conditions such as traumatic nerve injuries and peripheral neuropathy.</p></p>
