<p><p>The Proctology Examination Chairs market consists of various types, including Electropneumatic Examination Chairs and Electric Examination Chairs. Electropneumatic Examination Chairs are equipped with a combination of electrical and pneumatic mechanisms, allowing adjustments of height, backrest, and leg sections. These chairs provide flexibility and comfort during examination procedures. On the other hand, Electric Examination Chairs operate solely using an electrical system for height and position adjustments. These chairs offer convenient and precise positioning options for both the patient and the healthcare professional conducting the examination.</p></p>
