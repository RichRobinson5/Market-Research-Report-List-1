<p><p>The transparent holographic lamination film market includes two types: fully transparent and translucent films. Fully transparent holographic lamination films are completely see-through, allowing clear visibility of the underlying surface or print. They provide a glossy and vibrant holographic effect while maintaining transparency. On the other hand, translucent holographic lamination films allow light to pass through but diffuses it, creating a semi-transparent effect. This type of film provides a unique and appealing appearance, displaying holographic patterns while partially concealing the printed material underneath.</p></p>
