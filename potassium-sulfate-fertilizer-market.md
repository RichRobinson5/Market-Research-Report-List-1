<p><p>Potassium sulfate fertilizer market consists of two main types: potassium sulfate granules and potassium sulfate powder. Potassium sulfate granules are solid particles of fertilizer that are easy to apply and mix with soil, providing slow release of nutrients to plants. On the other hand, potassium sulfate powder is a finely ground form of the fertilizer that dissolves quickly in water, allowing for rapid nutrient absorption by plants. Both market types offer advantages in terms of convenience and nutrient availability, catering to different needs of farmers and gardeners.</p></p>
