<p><p>The sterile medical plastic packaging market can be categorized into two types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging refers to packaging materials that can easily bend and are lightweight, such as medical pouches and bags. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, includes materials that are firm and do not easily deform, such as plastic trays and containers. Both types of packaging are used to ensure the sterility of medical products and protect them from contamination, but they differ in their physical properties and applications.</p></p>
