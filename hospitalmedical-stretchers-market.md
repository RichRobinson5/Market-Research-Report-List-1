<p><p>The hospital/medical stretchers market consists of three main types: fixed stretchers, adjustable stretchers, and stretcher chairs. Fixed stretchers are designed with a fixed height and provide a stable platform for patients during transportation or medical procedures. Adjustable stretchers allow healthcare professionals to change the height, angle, and position of the stretcher to accommodate different medical requirements. Stretcher chairs are versatile, serving as both a chair and a stretcher, making them suitable for situations where patients need to be transported or seated depending on their condition. These three types cater to different healthcare needs in terms of stability, flexibility, and versatility.</p></p>
