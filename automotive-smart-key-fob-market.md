<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive smart key fob market consists of two main types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows the user to unlock or lock the vehicle without pressing any buttons on the fob. It operates based on proximity to the car, automatically sensing the fob's presence. On the other hand, RKES requires the user to press buttons on the fob to perform actions like unlocking or locking the car. Both systems enhance convenience and security by allowing easy access to the vehicle.</p></p>
