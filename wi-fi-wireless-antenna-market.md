<p><p>Wi-Fi wireless antenna market can be categorized into two main types: omni directional antennas and directional antennas. Omni directional antennas are designed to transmit and receive signals in all directions, providing a 360-degree coverage. They are commonly used in environments where multiple devices need to be connected simultaneously. On the other hand, directional antennas focus their signal in a specific direction, allowing for longer range and stronger signal quality. These antennas are often preferred for point-to-point communication and for connecting devices that are located at a distance from each other.</p></p>
