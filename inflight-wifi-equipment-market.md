<p><strong>The Inflight WIFI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATG</li><li>Ka Band Satellite</li><li>Ku Band Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inflight WIFI Equipment market comprises three main types: ATG (Air-to-Ground), Ka Band Satellite, and Ku Band Satellite. ATG systems provide WIFI connectivity using ground-based stations that communicate with the aircraft. Ka Band Satellite technologies use high-frequency signals transmitted by satellites to provide internet access during flights. Ku Band Satellite systems operate on a lower frequency range than Ka Band, enabling better penetration through bad weather conditions, ensuring uninterrupted connectivity. Each market type offers varying features and benefits, catering to different requirements of airlines and passengers for inflight WIFI services.</p></p>
