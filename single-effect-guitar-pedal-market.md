<p><p>Single effect guitar pedals are devices used to modify the sound of an electric guitar in various ways. Overdrive pedals create a smooth, natural breakup, while delay pedals introduce echo and repeats. Reverb pedals simulate different room acoustics, and chorus pedals add depth and width to the sound.
