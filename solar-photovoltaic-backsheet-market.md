<p><p>The Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Backsheet Market comprises various types of backsheet structures. KPK Structure refers to backsheets made of a combination of fluoropolymers, polyethylene terephthalate (PET), and a layer of adhesive. KPE Structure is similar to KPK, but with an added layer of ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA). TPE Structure involves using thermoplastic elastomer films. PPE Structure utilizes polyphenylene ether. Other Types include various materials used for backsheet production. These structures serve as protective layers for solar panels and enhance their performance, durability, and efficiency in converting sunlight into electricity.</p></p>
