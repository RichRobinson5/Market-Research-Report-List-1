<p><p>Public Safety Wireless Communication System market can be broadly categorized into two types: Analog and Digital. </p><p>Analog Public Safety Wireless Communication System utilizes analog signals to transmit voice and data. It is typically used by older systems and is known for its simplicity, lower cost, and compatibility with legacy infrastructure. However, it lacks advanced features and has limited capacity for simultaneous communication.</p><p>On the other hand, Digital Public Safety Wireless Communication System employs digital signals for improved voice clarity, enhanced data transmission, and increased capacity for simultaneous communication. It offers advanced features like encryption, text messaging, and GPS location tracking, making it more secure and efficient compared to analog systems.</p></p>
