<p><p>Asphaltene inhibitors and paraffin inhibitors are both types of chemicals used in the oil and gas industry to prevent the deposition and build-up of asphaltene and paraffin compounds, respectively. Asphaltene inhibitors work by dispersing and solubilizing asphaltene molecules, preventing them from precipitating and forming deposits that can block pipelines and equipment.
