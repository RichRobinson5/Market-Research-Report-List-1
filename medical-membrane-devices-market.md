<p><ul><li>Dialyzers</li><li>Blood Oxygenators</li><li>IV Filters</li><li>Blood Microfilters</li><li>Blood/Apheresis Filters</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical membrane devices are used in various medical procedures to separate and filter different components of blood or other fluids. Dialyzers are used in kidney dialysis to remove waste and excess fluid from the blood. Blood oxygenators are used to oxygenate and remove carbon dioxide from the blood during cardiac bypass surgeries. IV filters are used to remove impurities from intravenous fluids. Blood microfilters are used to remove small particles or pathogens from the blood. Blood/apheresis filters separate and collect specific blood components for therapeutic purposes. Other types of medical membrane devices include filters used in plasmapheresis or extracorporeal membrane oxygenation.</p></p>
