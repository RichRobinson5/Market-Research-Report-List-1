<p><p>Balloon ureteral dilators are medical devices used for ureteral dilation. These dilators consist of inflatable balloons attached to a catheter that are inserted into the ureter to expand the narrow areas and facilitate the passage of fluids, stones, or other obstructions. They are commonly used in various urological procedures, including the treatment of ureteral strictures, removal of kidney stones, and placement of ureteral stents.</p><p>The global balloon ureteral dilators market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The market analysis indicates that the rising prevalence of urological disorders, such as kidney stones and urinary tract infections, is one of the primary factors driving the market growth. Additionally, the increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures for urological interventions is further propelling the demand for balloon ureteral dilators.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in balloon technology and the development of innovative dilators with improved features are also contributing to the market growth.
