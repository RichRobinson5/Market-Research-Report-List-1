<p><p>Aeronautical Satcom refers to the use of satellite communications in the aviation industry. There are different types of satellite communication systems used, including L-Band, FSS Ku-Band, GEO-HTS Ku-Band, and GEO-HTS Ka-Band. </p><p>L-Band refers to low-frequency satellite signals, which provide reliable and global coverage. FSS Ku-Band stands for Fixed Satellite Service Ku-Band, offering higher bandwidth and improved performance for data transfer. GEO-HTS Ku-Band refers to Geostationary High Throughput Satellite in Ku-Band, offering even higher data transfer rates and capacity. Lastly, GEO-HTS Ka-Band refers to a similar technology but operates in Ka-Band frequency range, providing even greater bandwidth capabilities for aeronautical communications.</p></p>
