<p><strong>The Night-vision Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Night-vision goggles are used to enhance visibility in low-light or dark environments. There are two main types of night-vision goggles: image intensifier and thermal image goggles. Image intensifier goggles work by amplifying existing light, making objects more visible to the human eye. They are commonly used in military operations, search and rescue missions, and law enforcement activities. On the other hand, thermal image goggles use the heat emitted by objects to create an image. They are particularly useful for identifying heat signatures, such as humans or animals, in complete darkness or through thick vegetation.</p></p>
