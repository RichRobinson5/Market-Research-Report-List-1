<p><p>The hair mask market consists of various types, including steam-free hair masks and thermal steam hair masks. Steam-free hair masks do not require the use of steam or heat to activate the product. Instead, they are applied directly onto the hair and left to work their magic. On the other hand, thermal steam hair masks are designed to be used with heat. When applied onto the hair and exposed to heat, the mask's ingredients penetrate the hair shaft more effectively, providing deeper nourishment and hydration.</p></p>
