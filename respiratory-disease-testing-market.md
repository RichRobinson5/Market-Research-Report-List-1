<p><p>The Respiratory Disease Testing Market comprises various types of tests used to diagnose respiratory diseases. Imaging tests involve the use of techniques like X-rays, CT scans, and PET scans to visualize the respiratory system and identify abnormalities. Respiratory measurement tests assess lung function, such as spirometry or peak flow tests. Blood gas tests measure the levels of oxygen and carbon dioxide in the blood, aiding in the diagnosis of lung conditions. Other tests include genetic testing, sputum tests, and bronchoscopy, which assist in the identification and evaluation of respiratory diseases.</p></p>
