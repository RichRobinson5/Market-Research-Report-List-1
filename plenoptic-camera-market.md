<p><strong>The Plenoptic Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Focused Plenoptic Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plenoptic Camera market consists of two main types: Standard Plenoptic Camera and Focused Plenoptic Camera. Standard Plenoptic Cameras capture light and record its direction, allowing users to refocus the image after it is taken. These cameras provide a wide depth of field and are commonly used for various applications. On the other hand, Focused Plenoptic Cameras capture both directional and distance information of light, enabling users to refocus the image and adjust the depth of field even after capturing the photo. This type of camera is particularly useful in applications where selective focus is desired.</p></p>
