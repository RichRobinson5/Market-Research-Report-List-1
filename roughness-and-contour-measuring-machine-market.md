<p><ul><li>Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine</li><li>Non-Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roughness and contour measuring machine market can be categorized into two types: contact and non-contact machines. Contact roughness and contour measuring machines utilize a probe or stylus that physically touches the surface being measured to assess its roughness and contour. On the other hand, non-contact roughness and contour measuring machines employ optical sensors or lasers to capture surface data without any physical contact. Both types of machines play a crucial role in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace for quality control and dimensional analysis purposes.</p></p>
