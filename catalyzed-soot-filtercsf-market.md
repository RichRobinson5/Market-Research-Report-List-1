<p><p>The Catalyzed Soot Filter (CSF) is a type of filter used in diesel engines to reduce particulate matter emissions. There are two main types of CSF on the market: cordierite catalyzed soot filters and ceramic catalyzed soot filters. Cordierite filters are made from a porous material that traps and burns off soot particles, while ceramic filters are made from a ceramic material coated with a catalyst that helps to facilitate the soot oxidation process. Both types of filters help to improve air quality by reducing harmful emissions from diesel engines.</p></p>
