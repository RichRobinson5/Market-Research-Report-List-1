<p><p>Self-monitoring blood glucose strips are used by diabetic individuals to measure their blood sugar levels. These strips come in different types, including glucose oxidase and glucose dehydrogenase. The glucose oxidase strips use an enzyme called glucose oxidase to react with glucose and produce an electric current, which is then measured to determine the blood glucose level. Similarly, glucose dehydrogenase strips use an enzyme called glucose dehydrogenase to catalyze the reaction with glucose. Other types of strips may use different technologies or enzymes to achieve the same purpose of accurate blood sugar measurement.</p></p>
