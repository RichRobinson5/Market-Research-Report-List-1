<p><p>The hybrid loaders market comprises three types: hybrid wheel loaders, skid steer loaders, and track loaders. Hybrid wheel loaders are vehicles used for lifting and carrying materials, equipped with both an electric motor and an internal combustion engine for power. Skid steer loaders are compact machines with four wheels that can rotate independently, widely used in construction and landscaping tasks. Track loaders, on the other hand, are similar to skid steer loaders but have tracks instead of wheels for improved traction. These machines offer increased efficiency and reduced fuel consumption compared to traditional loaders.</p></p>
