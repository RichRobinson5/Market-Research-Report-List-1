The market for LCMS is categorized into three main types. Single Quadrupole LC-MS offers qualitative analysis, while Triple Quadrupole LC-MS provides both quantitative and qualitative analysis, making it suitable for trace detection. Ion Trap LC-MS combines the capabilities of both single and triple quadrupole systems, allowing for an increased range of analysis and greater sensitivity.</p></p>
