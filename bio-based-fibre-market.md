<p><p>The bio-based fiber market is divided into two main types: plant fiber and animal fiber. Plant fiber refers to fibers derived from plants, such as cotton, hemp, and flax. Animal fibers are valued for their softness, warmth, and good insulation properties. Both types of fibers are widely used in various industries, including textiles, fashion, and home furnishings.</p></p>
