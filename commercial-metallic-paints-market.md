<p><p>Commercial metallic paints are available in two types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic paints use solvents as a base component to dissolve and disperse the metallic pigments, providing excellent durability and resistance to harsh weather conditions. On the other hand, water-based metallic paints use water as a base component instead of solvents, making them more environmentally friendly and less toxic. Water-based paints offer easy clean-up and emit fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs) compared to solvent-based paints.</p></p>
