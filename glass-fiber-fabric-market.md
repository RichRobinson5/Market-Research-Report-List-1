<p><p>The glass-fiber fabric market includes three main types: alkali-free glass fiber, medium-alkali glass fiber, and high-alkali glass fiber. Alkali-free glass fiber is manufactured without alkali oxides, making it suitable for applications requiring high chemical resistance. Medium-alkali glass fiber has a moderate amount of alkali oxides, offering a balance between chemical resistance and cost-effectiveness. High-alkali glass fiber contains a higher proportion of alkali oxides, providing excellent chemical resistance but at a higher cost. These different types cater to diverse industrial needs, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable option for their specific application.</p></p>
