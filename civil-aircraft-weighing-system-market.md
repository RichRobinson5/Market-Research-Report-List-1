<p><p>The civil aircraft weighing system market offers two types of weighing platforms, namely, platform and floor-standing. Platform weighing systems are designed with a flat surface that can hold the aircraft easily, ensuring accurate weight measurement. On the other hand, floor-standing weighing systems are equipped with built-in ramps for aircraft to move onto the weighing platform, offering convenience for larger aircraft. Both types of weighing systems play a crucial role in ensuring aircraft safety and compliance with weight limitations.</p></p>
