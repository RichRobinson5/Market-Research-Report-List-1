<p><p>The Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) hardware market encompasses various types of equipment used in NMR imaging and spectroscopy. This includes magnets that generate the main magnetic field, field lock systems that stabilize this field, shim coils that correct any inhomogeneities in the field, sample probes that hold the sample being analyzed, RF coils that transmit and receive radiofrequency signals, gradient coils that create spatial encoding to produce images, and quadrature detectors that improve signal reception. These hardware components play crucial roles in NMR systems, enabling accurate and high-quality imaging and spectroscopy.</p></p>
