<p><p>Water Based Lithography Inks are a type of printing ink that uses water as a solvent instead of traditional petroleum-based solvents. <p><p>Water-based lithography inks are widely used in the printing industry and can be categorized into three types: Adagio printing, silk screen printing, and digital printing. Adagio printing uses a water-based ink that allows for high-quality prints and vibrant colors. Silk screen printing employs a stencil-based technique, where a mesh screen is used to transfer ink onto the printing surface. Digital printing utilizes computer technology to directly transfer the image onto the printing substrate. These different types cater to various printing requirements and provide versatile options for businesses in the market.</p></p>
