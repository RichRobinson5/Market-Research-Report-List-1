<p><p>High heat melamine foam refers to a type of foam material that can withstand high temperatures. There are three main types of this foam in the market: rigid, semi-rigid, and flexible. Rigid melamine foam is the most dense and sturdy, suitable for applications that require strong insulation properties. Semi-rigid melamine foam offers a balance between rigidity and flexibility, making it ideal for applications that require moderate insulation and shapeability. Flexible melamine foam is the least dense and most pliable, commonly used for soundproofing and acoustic insulation purposes.</p></p>
