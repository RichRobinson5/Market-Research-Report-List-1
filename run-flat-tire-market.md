<p><ul><li>Self-Supporting</li><li>Support Ring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Run-flat tires are a type of tires designed to resist deflation in the event of a puncture, enabling the vehicle to continue driving. There are two main types of run-flat tires: self-supporting and support ring system. Self-supporting run-flat tires have reinforced sidewalls that can support the weight of the vehicle even with no air pressure, allowing the driver to continue for a certain distance at reduced speeds. Meanwhile, support ring system run-flat tires utilize a mechanical ring fitted onto the wheel, providing support to the vehicle's weight in case of a flat tire.</p></p>
