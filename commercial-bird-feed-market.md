<p><p>In the commercial bird feed market, there are two primary types: staple and supplement. Staple bird feed refers to the primary source of nutrition for birds, containing essential ingredients such as grains, seeds, and pellets. It provides a balanced diet and is intended to be the main food for the birds. On the other hand, supplement bird feed enhances the diet by providing additional nutrients, flavors, or textures. It is meant to be fed alongside the staple bird feed, offering variety and catering to specific dietary needs of different bird species.</p></p>
