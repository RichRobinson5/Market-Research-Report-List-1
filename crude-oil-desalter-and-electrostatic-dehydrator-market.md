<p><p>A crude oil desalter is a device used in the oil refining process to remove unwanted substances, such as salts and water, from crude oil. It helps in improving the quality of crude oil before it undergoes further processing. On the other hand, an electrostatic dehydrator is a piece of equipment used to remove water from crude oil using electric fields. It is an efficient and environmentally friendly alternative to traditional methods of dehydration.</p><p>The crude oil desalter and electrostatic dehydrator market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for refined products, coupled with the need for high-quality crude oil, is driving the market.
