<p><strong>The Nano Silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P type</li><li>S type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nano silica, also known as silicon dioxide, is a compound that is widely used in various industries. It is classified into two types: P type and S type. P type nano silica is produced from amorphous quartz, which contains impurities like aluminum and boron. It is commonly used in the rubber industry to enhance its strength and durability. On the other hand, S type nano silica is synthesized from quartzite and is much purer. It is primarily utilized in the construction industry to improve the mechanical properties of concrete and increase its resistance to cracking.</p></p>
