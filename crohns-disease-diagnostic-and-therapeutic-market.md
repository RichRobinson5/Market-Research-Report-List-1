<p><p>The Crohn's Disease Diagnostic and Therapeutic Market includes various types of tests and procedures used for diagnosis and treatment. Colonoscopy and flexible sigmoidoscopy involve inserting a tube with a camera into the colon to visualize and biopsy affected areas. Computerized Tomography (CT) and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) create detailed images of the digestive tract. Capsule endoscopy utilizes a pill-sized camera to capture images while passing through the digestive system.</p></p>
