The B Type MCBs are suitable for general domestic and commercial applications, providing protection against overloads and short circuits. C Type MCBs are designed for applications with higher inrush currents, such as motor loads. They offer faster tripping times to prevent damage. D Type MCBs are specifically designed for applications with highly inductive loads, like transformers, where high inrush currents are expected. They provide even faster tripping times to protect the circuit effectively.</p></p>
