<p><p>Photolithography agents are used in the semiconductor industry to create patterns on silicon wafer surfaces during manufacturing processes. There are five main market types in photolithography agents: ArF Immersion, ArF Dry, KrF, I-Line, and G-Line. ArF Immersion involves using water to enhance resolution. ArF Dry is a dry method with good depth of focus. KrF is an older technology with larger feature sizes. I-Line is another older technology with larger feature sizes than ArF. G-Line is the oldest technology with the largest feature sizes.</p></p>
