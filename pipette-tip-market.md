<p><p>The pipette tip market is comprised of two main types: filtered and non-filtered pipette tips. Filtered pipette tips have integrated filters that prevent the contamination of samples and pipettes, ensuring accurate and reliable results. These tips are commonly used in molecular biology, genomics, and cell culture applications. Non-filtered pipette tips, on the other hand, do not have any filtration system and are suitable for general laboratory tasks where the risk of contamination is lower. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs of the experiment or task at hand.</p></p>
