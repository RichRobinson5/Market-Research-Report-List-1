<p><p>The market for digital fabric printing inks includes various types such as reactive dye inks, acidic inks, paint inks, dispersion inks, and sublimation inks. Reactive dye inks are commonly used for natural fabrics and provide vibrant colors with excellent color fastness. Acidic inks are suitable for synthetic fabrics and offer good lightfastness. Paint inks are used for printing on both natural and synthetic fabrics and provide a glossy finish. Dispersion inks are ideal for direct-to-garment printing, while sublimation inks are used for transfer printing and provide vibrant and long-lasting colors.</p></p>
