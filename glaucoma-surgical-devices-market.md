<p><p>The Glaucoma Surgical Devices Market comprises two main types of devices: Glaucoma Drainage Devices and Glaucoma Laser Systems. Glaucoma Drainage Devices are implantable devices that help to regulate the flow of fluid within the eye, reducing intraocular pressure. On the other hand, Glaucoma Laser Systems are used to treat glaucoma by using lasers to modify the drainage system of the eye, improving fluid outflow and lowering intraocular pressure. Both types of devices aim to manage and alleviate the symptoms of glaucoma, a condition characterized by increased eye pressure that can lead to vision loss if left untreated.</p></p>
