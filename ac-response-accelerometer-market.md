<p><p>The AC response accelerometer market encompasses three main types: 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis accelerometers. Each type measures acceleration along different directions. A 1-axis accelerometer detects acceleration along one specific axis, typically vertical or horizontal. Meanwhile, a 2-axis accelerometer measures acceleration in two planes, usually both horizontal directions. Lastly, a 3-axis accelerometer detects acceleration in three perpendicular axes, providing comprehensive measurement capability along all three dimensions. These different types cater to various applications that require monitoring and analysis of acceleration in specific directions or across multiple planes.</p></p>
