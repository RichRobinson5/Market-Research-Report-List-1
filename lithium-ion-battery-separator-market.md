<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The lithium-ion battery separator market can be classified into three types: Single-Layer, Bilayer, and Trilayer. The Single-Layer separator is made from either polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE) materials. Bilayer separators can be composed of PP/PE or PP/PP layers. Trilayer separators consist of PP/PE/PP layers. These separators play a crucial role in preventing short circuits and enabling the efficient flow of ions between battery electrodes. Each type offers different performance characteristics, making them suitable for various applications in the lithium-ion battery market.</p></p>
