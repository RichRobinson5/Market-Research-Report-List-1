<p><p>Cast polymers, also known as artificial or engineered stone, are a type of composite material made by blending natural stone chips or marble dust with a polymer resin. Cast polymers offer various advantages over natural stone, including enhanced durability, ease of maintenance, and a wide range of color options.</p><p>The cast polymers market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue this trend during the forecast period. <p><p>The Cast Polymers market encompasses various types including Aluminum Hydroxide, Calcium Carbonate, Resin, and Quartz. Aluminum Hydroxide is commonly used as a filler material due to its fire-resistant properties. Calcium Carbonate is chosen for its cost-effectiveness and ability to improve the mechanical properties of cast polymers. Resin, which can be polyester or acrylic, provides strength and durability. Quartz, known for its hardness and visual appeal, is used as a decorative material in cast polymers.
