<p><p>Friction Stir Welding (FSW) machine is a technique used to join two or more materials by using frictional heat, mechanical pressure, and plastic deformation. It is a solid-state joining process that produces a high-quality, defect-free weld without the need for filler materials or post-weld treatments. The FSW machine consists of a rotating tool that generates heat by frictional rubbing against the materials, while simultaneously applying pressure to form a joint.</p><p>The current outlook of the FSW machine market is positive, with increasing adoption in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, shipbuilding, and railways. The advantages of FSW, such as improved weld quality, reduced distortion, and enhanced mechanical properties, have resulted in its growing popularity among manufacturers. Moreover, the demand for lightweight and high-strength materials, such as aluminum and magnesium alloys, has further propelled the market growth.</p><p>In terms of the future outlook, the FSW machine market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecasted period.
