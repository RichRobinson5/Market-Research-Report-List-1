<p><p>The minimally invasive surgical instrument market consists of various types of instruments used in minimally invasive surgeries. Electrosurgical instruments are used for cutting and coagulating tissues. Handheld instruments include forceps, graspers, and scissors that are manipulated by surgeons during procedures. Inflation systems assist in creating and maintaining a working space inside the body. Auxiliary instruments aid in holding, retracting, or suturing tissues. Cutter instruments, such as staplers and drills, are used for tissue removal. Guiding devices assist in navigating and positioning instruments during surgery.</p></p>
