<p><p>The Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) Balloon Catheter market consists of various types: Semi Compliant, Non Compliant, and Compliant. Semi Compliant balloon catheters are designed to expand and adapt to the shape of blood vessels, allowing for controlled dilation. Non Compliant balloon catheters have a rigid structure, providing higher pressure expansion for calcified or resistant plaques. Compliant balloon catheters are flexible and can be easily inflated, ideal for delicate lesions. These different types cater to diverse clinical scenarios, ensuring effective treatment of coronary artery diseases during PCI procedures.</p></p>
