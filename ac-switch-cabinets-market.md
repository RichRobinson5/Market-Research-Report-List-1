<p><p>AC switch cabinets are control devices used in electrical power distribution systems to regulate the flow of electricity. High Voltage Switch Cabinets are designed to handle high voltage levels, typically above 1000V, and are commonly used in large-scale power plants and transmission and distribution networks. On the other hand, Low Voltage Switch Cabinets are intended for lower voltage levels, usually below 1000V, and are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. Both types of switch cabinets serve the purpose of protecting electrical systems, controlling power flow, and ensuring safety and efficiency in the distribution of electricity.</p></p>
