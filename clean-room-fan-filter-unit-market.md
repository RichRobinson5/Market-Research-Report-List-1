<p><p>The Clean Room Fan Filter Unit market consists of two main types: Fan Filter Units (FFUs) equipped with High Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters and FFUs with Ultra Low Particulate Air (ULPA) filters. HEPA FFUs are designed to remove a high percentage of airborne particles, typically 99.97% of particles sized 0.3 micrometers or larger. ULPA FFUs, on the other hand, are even more efficient, removing particles sized 0.1 micrometers or larger at a filtration rate of 99.999%. Both types are widely used in clean room environments to ensure a high level of air cleanliness.</p></p>
