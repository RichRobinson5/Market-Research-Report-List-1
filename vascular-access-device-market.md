<p><strong>The Vascular Access Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Vascular Access Devices</li><li>Peripheral Vascular Access Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vascular Access Device market consists of two main types: Central Vascular Access Devices and Peripheral Vascular Access Devices. Central Vascular Access Devices are used to access large central veins, such as the superior vena cava, and are often used for long-term treatments or administration of medications. Peripheral Vascular Access Devices, on the other hand, are inserted into peripheral veins, such as those found in the arms, and are typically used for short-term treatments or for accessing veins in patients with difficult venous access.</p></p>
