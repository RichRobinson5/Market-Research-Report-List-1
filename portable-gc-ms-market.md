<p><p>Portable GC-MS, or portable gas chromatography-mass spectrometry, refers to a compact and lightweight analytical instrument that combines the capabilities of gas chromatography and mass spectrometry in a portable format. The growing demand for portable and field-deployable analytical instruments, coupled with advancements in miniaturization technologies, is driving the market growth. The market is being fueled by increasing concerns regarding environmental pollution, stringent government regulations related to food and drug safety, and the need for rapid and reliable on-site analysis.
