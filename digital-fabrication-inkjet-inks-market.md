<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Digital fabrication inkjet inks can be categorized into two types based on their composition: dye-based inks and pigment-based inks. Dye-based inks contain colorants that are dissolved in a liquid, providing vibrant and intense colors. They are commonly used for applications requiring high color accuracy but may fade over time. Pigment-based inks, on the other hand, contain solid particles suspended in a liquid medium. They provide excellent lightfastness and are suitable for applications where long-lasting and fade-resistant prints are required.</p></p>
