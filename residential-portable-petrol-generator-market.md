<p><p>A residential portable petrol generator, also known as a backup generator or emergency generator, is a compact and mobile power generation device that runs on petrol fuel. These generators are designed to be easily transported to different locations and are typically smaller in size compared to commercial generators.</p><p>The residential portable petrol generator market has witnessed steady growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding in the foreseeable future. The increasing frequency of power outages caused by natural disasters, aging infrastructure, and the growing demand for uninterrupted power supply in residential areas are key drivers fueling the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the rising popularity of outdoor recreational activities such as camping, tailgating, and boating has also contributed to the market growth. Residential portable petrol generators are widely used in these activities to power various electrical appliances and equipment.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have led to the development of more fuel-efficient and environmentally-friendly generators, which further drives the market growth.
