<p><p>Stand Guidance System in airports refers to tools or technologies used to guide aircraft during parking or docking procedures. There are two main types of Stand Guidance Systems: Visual Docking Guidance System (VDGS) and Advanced Visual Docking Guidance System (A-VDGS). VDGS utilizes visual cues, such as lights and markings, to assist pilots in aligning the aircraft with the parking stand. A-VDGS, on the other hand, incorporates additional features like sensors and displays to provide more precise guidance and enhance safety during the docking process.</p></p>
