<p><p>The automotive elastomer market encompasses various types of elastomers. These include natural rubber, which offers high elasticity and excellent resilience. SBR (styrene-butadiene rubber) is a synthetic elastomer offering good abrasion resistance. Nitrile elastomer is known for its resistance to oil and fuel. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) offers superior weather resistance. Silicone rubber provides excellent heat resistance. Fluoroelastomer is highly resistant to chemicals and extreme temperatures. Styrene block copolymers offer good heat and oil resistance. Lastly, other elastomers include polyurethane and chloroprene rubber, each with their own unique properties.</p></p>
