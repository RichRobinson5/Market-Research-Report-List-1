<p><p>Dental steam generators are used in dental offices for sterilizing instruments and equipment. There are two types of dental steam generators in the market: integral boiler and remote boiler. Integral boiler steam generators have the boiler and generator combined in one unit. They are compact and require less space. On the other hand, remote boiler steam generators have a separate boiler and generator. They are ideal for larger dental clinics as they provide higher steam capacities. Both types are essential in maintaining a hygienic environment in dental practices.</p></p>
