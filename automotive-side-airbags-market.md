<p><p>Automotive side airbags are designed to provide additional protection to passengers in the event of a side impact collision. There are two main types of side airbags: side torso airbags and side tubular or curtain airbags. Side torso airbags are typically located in the seatbacks or door panels and provide protection for the upper body and vital organs. Side tubular or curtain airbags, on the other hand, are installed along the roofline or window area and offer protection for both the head and torso, creating a protective barrier between the occupants and the side of the vehicle.</p></p>
