<p><p>The automotive air filter cartridge market consists of two main types: polyester fiber and wood pulp fiber. Polyester fiber filters are known for their durability and ability to trap small particles, such as dust and pollen. They are popular in areas with high pollution levels. On the other hand, wood pulp fiber filters are more cost-effective and environmentally friendly. They are effective in capturing larger particles like debris and insects. Both types play a vital role in improving air quality and protecting car engines from harmful contaminants.</p></p>
