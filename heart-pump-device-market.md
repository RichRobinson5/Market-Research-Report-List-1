<p><p>The heart pump device market consists of three main types: Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs), Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs), and Total Artificial Heart (TAH) systems. VADs are implanted mechanical devices that help the heart pump blood to the body when it is weakened or diseased. IABPs are temporary devices inserted into the aorta to enhance blood flow and reduce the workload on the heart. TAH systems completely replace the failing heart by implanting an artificial heart into the patient's chest, providing both ventricular support and circulation. These devices play a crucial role in supporting and improving heart function in patients with various cardiac conditions.</p></p>
