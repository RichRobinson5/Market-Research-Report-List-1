<p><p>The solar panels market can be categorized into two main types: Crystalline Silicon Solar Panel and Thin-Film Solar Panel. Crystalline Silicon Solar Panels are made from silicon wafers and are the most common and widely used type. They offer high efficiency and durability, making them suitable for residential and commercial installations. On the other hand, Thin-Film Solar Panels use a thin photovoltaic material deposited onto a substrate and are more flexible and lightweight. They are often used in large-scale solar power plants and for applications where flexibility is required.</p></p>
