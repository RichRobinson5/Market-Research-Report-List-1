<p><p>Aero Engines can be classified into two main market types: piston engines and gas turbine engines. Piston engines, also known as reciprocating engines, use reciprocating motion to convert fuel combustion into linear mechanical motion. These engines are commonly used in smaller aircraft such as light planes and helicopters. On the other hand, gas turbine engines, also referred to as jet engines, utilize the expansion of hot gases to generate thrust. These engines are widely employed in commercial airliners and military aircraft, offering high power output and efficiency.</p></p>
