<p><p>Self-adhesive protective films are a type of temporary coating applied to surfaces to prevent damage during storage, transportation, or installation. They can be classified into different categories based on the material used, such as PP (polypropylene) and PE (polyethylene). PP films offer excellent clarity, durability, and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, PE films are known for their flexibility, impact resistance, and moisture resistance, making them ideal for protecting surfaces in outdoor or high-moisture environments. Both PP and PE films provide reliable protection and are widely used in industries like construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
