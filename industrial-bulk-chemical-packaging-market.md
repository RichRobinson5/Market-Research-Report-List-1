<p><p>The industrial bulk chemical packaging market comprises various types of packaging options, including drums, flexitanks, intermediate bulk containers (IBCs), and others. Drums are cylindrical containers made of steel or plastic, commonly used for storing and transporting chemicals. Flexitanks are large, flexible bags that can hold liquids and are often used for transporting bulk liquids. IBCs are rigid containers with a capacity typically between 200 and 3,000 liters, designed for the storage and transportation of liquid and granular materials. "Others" refer to alternative packaging options such as bulk bags or pails commonly used in specific industries.</p></p>
