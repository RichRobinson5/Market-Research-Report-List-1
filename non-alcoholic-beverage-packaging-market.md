<p><p>The non-alcoholic beverage packaging market consists of two main types: rigid packaging and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging refers to the use of sturdy materials like glass or plastic bottles, aluminum cans, or paperboard cartons. It provides durability and protection to preserve the beverage's quality. On the other hand, flexible packaging involves the use of materials such as plastic films or pouches that can be easily folded or bent. It offers convenience, portability, and often comes with resealable features. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and market demands in the beverage industry.</p></p>
