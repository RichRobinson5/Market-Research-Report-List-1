<p><p>The automotive thermoplastic polymer composites market is divided into different types based on the manufacturing process used. Compression molding applies heat and pressure to the polymer composite material in a mold to form solid parts. Other manufacturing processes in this market include blow molding and thermoforming. These various techniques cater to different automotive manufacturing requirements and provide a diverse range of thermoplastic polymer composites products.</p></p>
