<p><p>The Monoclonal Antibody Diagnostic Reagents Market includes various types of diagnostic assays. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is a common method that uses an enzyme-labeled antibody to detect specific antigen-antibody interactions. Dot-immunogold filtration assay involves the use of gold-labeled antibodies that bind to target antigens and are later detected by filtration. Double Antigen Sandwich Chemiluminescence Method employs two monoclonal antibodies to form a sandwich complex around the target antigen, and chemiluminescent detection is used for quantification. Recombinant Immunoblot Assay is a technique that utilizes purified recombinant antigens to detect specific antibodies in patient samples.</p></p>
