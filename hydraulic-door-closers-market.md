<p><p>Hydraulic door closers are devices used to automatically close doors, providing convenience and safety. There are three types of hydraulic door closers: the surface-applied door closer, concealed door closer, and floor spring. A surface-applied door closer is mounted on the surface of the door and frame, visible to the user. A concealed door closer is installed within the door frame, remaining hidden from sight. The floor spring is a hydraulic device installed at the bottom of the door, controlling its movement. These different types offer various installation options to suit different architectural and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
