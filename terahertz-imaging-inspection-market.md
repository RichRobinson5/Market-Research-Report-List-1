<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection market is categorized into two types: Passive Terahertz Imaging and Active Terahertz Imaging. Passive Terahertz Imaging utilizes the naturally occurring Terahertz radiation emitted by objects to create images. It does not require external stimulation and is ideal for noninvasive applications like security screening. On the other hand, Active Terahertz Imaging involves the use of a Terahertz source to emit radiation, which interacts with objects and generates images. This method enables better image resolution and is commonly used for industrial inspection, medical diagnosis, and research purposes.</p></p>
