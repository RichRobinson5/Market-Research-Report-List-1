<p><p>The PC-TV tuner market consists of various types, namely USB and TapC. USB tuners are compact devices that can be easily plugged into a computer's USB port. They enable users to receive TV signals on their PCs and watch live TV shows. On the other hand, TapC tuners, also known as television access point cards, are expansion cards that need to be inserted into a card slot on the computer's motherboard. Like USB tuners, they allow users to watch TV on their PCs, but require internal installation.</p></p>
