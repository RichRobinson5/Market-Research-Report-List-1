<p><strong>The Mass Flow Regulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mass flow regulators are devices designed to control and regulate the flow rate of gases or fluids. They can be classified into two main types: direct type and indirect type.</p><p>Direct type mass flow regulators directly measure and control the flow rate of gases or fluids passing through them. They operate based on the principle of the conservation of mass, ensuring accurate and precise flow control.</p><p>Indirect type mass flow regulators, on the other hand, indirectly measure and control the flow rate by utilizing feedback control systems. They use sensors and controllers to measure and adjust the flow rate, offering a more sophisticated and adjustable flow control solution.</p><p>Both direct and indirect types of mass flow regulators are widely used in industries such as pharmaceutical, semiconductor, and chemical, where precise and accurate flow control is required.</p></p>
