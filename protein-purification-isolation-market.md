<p><p>Protein purification is a method used to isolate and separate proteins from a complex mixture. The market for protein purification products is classified into various types based on the techniques used. Ultrafiltration involves using a membrane to separate proteins based on size. Precipitation involves the addition of chemicals to cause proteins to precipitate out of solution. Centrifugation involves spinning samples to separate proteins based on density. Chromatography utilizes a stationary phase and a mobile phase to separate proteins based on their interactions. Electrophoresis separates proteins based on their charge. Other techniques include magnetic separation and affinity purification.</p></p>
