<p><strong>The Fume Hoods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Hoods</li><li>Ducted Fume Hoods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fume hoods are essential in laboratories to ensure safety by containing and removing hazardous fumes and gases. Ductless fume hoods are self-contained units equipped with filters to remove contaminants before releasing air back into the room. They offer flexibility as they can be placed anywhere in the lab. On the other hand, ducted fume hoods are connected to an exhaust system, allowing captured fumes to be directed outside the building. They offer higher containment as fumes are completely removed. Both types have their advantages and selection depends on specific lab requirements.</p></p>
