<p><p>Nanofiltration membrane market types can be categorized as polymeric, inorganic, and hybrid. Polymeric membranes are made of synthetic polymers and are widely used due to their flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Inorganic membranes are composed of inorganic materials like ceramics or metals and offer excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability. Hybrid membranes combine both polymeric and inorganic materials, resulting in improved filtration performance and durability. These different types cater to various industrial applications where nanofiltration is required for separation, purification, or concentration processes.</p></p>
