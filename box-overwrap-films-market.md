<p><p>Box overwrap films are used to wrap and protect boxes during transportation and storage. There are two main types of box overwrap films: shrink films and stretch films. Shrink films are made of a material that shrinks when subjected to heat, tightly conforming to the shape of the box. This provides a secure and tamper-evident packaging solution. On the other hand, stretch films are elastic and can be stretched around the box, offering a tight and durable packaging solution. Both types of films play a crucial role in ensuring the safe transportation of boxed goods.</p></p>
