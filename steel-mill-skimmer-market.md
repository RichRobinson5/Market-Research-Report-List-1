<p><strong>The Steel Mill Skimmer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Type</li><li>Disc Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel mill skimmer market comprises two main types: belt type and disc type. Belt type skimmers are equipped with a continuous flat belt that collects and removes floating oil from the surface of the water. On the other hand, disc type skimmers utilize a series of rotating discs to skim the oil. Both types are effective in oil removal applications, with the choice depending on factors such as the volume of oil to be skimmed, the type of oil, and the specific requirements of the steel mill.</p></p>
