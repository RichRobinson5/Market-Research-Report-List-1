<p><p>Swimming pool disinfection equipment market types include chlorine dosing equipment, ozonation disinfection equipment, ultraviolet (UV) disinfection equipment, electrolysis disinfection equipment, and other equipment. Ultraviolet (UV) disinfection equipment utilizes UV light to destroy microorganisms in the water. Electrolysis disinfection equipment employs the process of electrolysis to create disinfectants and sanitize pool water. Lastly, other disinfection equipment refers to alternative technologies or methods used for pool sanitization.</p></p>
