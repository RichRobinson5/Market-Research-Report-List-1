<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Metal detectors for frozen food market are available in three types - pulse induction, very low frequency, and beat-frequency oscillation. Pulse induction metal detectors are effective for detecting non-ferrous and ferrous metals in contaminated products. Very low frequency detectors provide higher sensitivity to smaller metal contaminants and are commonly used in the food industry. Beat-frequency oscillation detectors use two coils to create a beat frequency when a metal contaminant is detected, enabling accurate identification and separation of contaminated products in frozen food production.</p></p>
