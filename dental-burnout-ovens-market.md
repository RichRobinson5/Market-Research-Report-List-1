<p><p>Dental burnout ovens are used in the dental industry to eliminate wax or other organic materials from molds before casting dental restorations. There are different types of dental burnout ovens available in the market. Muffle burnout ovens use traditional heating elements to produce heat within a closed chamber. Vacuum burnout ovens evacuate air from the chamber, ensuring a clean and precise burnout process. Infrared burnout ovens use infrared radiation to generate heat. Microwave burnout ovens utilize microwaves to rapidly heat the molds. Other types of burnout ovens may include unique technologies or a combination of different heating methods.</p></p>
