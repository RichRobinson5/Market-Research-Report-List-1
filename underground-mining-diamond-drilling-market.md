<p><p>The underground mining diamond drilling market comprises two main types: rotary drilling and wireline drilling. Rotary drilling involves the use of a rotating drill bit that crushes the rock, creating a hole. This type of drilling is commonly used in softer formations. On the other hand, wireline drilling deploys a wireline system to extract rock samples from the drill hole. It is preferred for harder rock formations and provides quicker and more efficient sampling. Both methods play a crucial role in underground mining operations, aiding in exploration and resource evaluation.</p></p>
