<p><p>Electrochemical-based devices refer to a range of instruments utilized in various industries for analyzing and measuring different parameters. Titrators are devices used to determine the concentration of a substance in a solution. Electrochemical meters are used to measure parameters such as pH, conductivity, and dissolved oxygen in a solution. Ion chromatographs are specialized devices used for separating and analyzing ions in a sample. Potentiostats are instruments used to control and measure the potential between an electrode and a solution. Other devices in this market include electrochemical analyzers and sensors used for various applications such as environmental monitoring, food analysis, and pharmaceutical research.</p></p>
