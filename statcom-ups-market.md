<p><p>STATCOM UPS refers to Static Synchronous Compensator Uninterruptible Power Supply, used for electrical power supply stability. Two main types of STATCOM UPS exist: Gate Turn-Off Thyristors (GTO) and Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors (IGBT) STATCOM. GTO STATCOM utilizes gate turn-off thyristors technology to control reactive power in electrical grids. On the other hand, IGBT STATCOM employs insulated gate bipolar transistor technology for power control applications. Both types enhance grid stability and power quality while ensuring uninterrupted power supply.</p></p>
